Course Overview

Principles of Cyber Security: This course provides an in-depth understanding of cybersecurity principles, focusing on encryption, network security, and threat prevention. Topics include cryptographic techniques, firewall configurations, intrusion detection systems, and ethical hacking concepts. Students explore how cyber threats evolve and how security professionals defend against attacks such as ransomware, denial-of-service (DoS) attacks, and data breaches. The course also examines security policies, risk management, and compliance with industry standards. By the end, students will have the foundational knowledge needed for careers in cybersecurity and ethical hacking.

Noteworthy Assignments/Projects

  • Encryption Implementation: Develop a Python-based encryption tool using AES or RSA.
  • Penetration Testing Report: Conduct security testing on a mock system and document vulnerabilities and solutions.
