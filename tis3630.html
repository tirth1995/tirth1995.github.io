Course Overview

Business Info Systems: This course explores the role of information systems in modern business environments. It covers database management, enterprise resource planning (ERP), and decision support systems. Students learn how businesses use technology to manage operations, analyze data, and improve efficiency. Key topics include database design, SQL, cloud computing, and business analytics. The course also highlights how organizations leverage data-driven insights to make strategic decisions, enhance customer relationships, and optimize supply chain management.

Noteworthy Assignments/Projects

  • Database Design Project: Creating an ER diagram and implementing a relational database.
  • Business Analytics Report: Analyzing company data to find trends and generate insights.
