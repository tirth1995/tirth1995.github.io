This course focuses on online safety, digital privacy, and best practices for securing personal and professional data. Topics include password management, two-factor authentication, phishing awareness, and secure browsing techniques. Students also learn about malware threats, identity theft prevention, and methods to safely use social media and online services. By the end of the course, students will have a solid understanding of cybersecurity fundamentals and how to protect themselves in an increasingly interconnected world.

Noteworthy Assignments/Projects

  • Secure Password Manager: Develop a basic password manager that encrypts and stores credentials securely.
